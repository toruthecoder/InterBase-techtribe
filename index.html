More About InterBase

InterBase® is afull-featured, high performance, ultrafast, encryptable, scalable, relational and embeddable multi-platform
SQL database with multi-dimensional data security, disaster recovery and change synchronization for developers who
want to embed a low-cost, zero-admin, secure database into their cross-platform connected applications.

InterBase® is one of the first enterprise-grade relational databases ever produced. Since its inception it is based on a
superior versioning architecture still unmatched by many popular databases. It was also the first DBMS to implement
Multi-Version Concurrency Control (MVCC). MVCC is referred to as Multi-Generational Architecture in InterBase®.
